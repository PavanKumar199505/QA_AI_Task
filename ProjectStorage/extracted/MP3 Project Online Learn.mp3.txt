project, online learning platform, overview. We are building an online learning platform to provide users with access to educational courses. The platform should allow users to register, browse courses, enroll in courses, and track their progress. Business requirements. Increase user engagement by providing interactive content. Ensure the platform is accessible on both web and mobile devices. Support multiple languages for global reach, functional requirements, user registration and login system, course catalog with search and filter options, course enrollment and progress tracking, video playback for course content, quizzes and assessments after each module, non-functional requirements. The platform should load pages within two seconds, support at least 10,000 concurrent users, ensure data privacy and security compliance with GDPR.
